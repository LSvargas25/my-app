Project Overview
Fit House is a large-scale gym management system designed to address the complex needs of modern fitness centers. Developed as a university thesis, the project integrates inventory control, user and client management, workout routines, and administrative modules into a single, scalable platform. Its architecture and feature set reflect a deep commitment to engineering best practices and real-world applicability, making it fully prepared for commercial use.
