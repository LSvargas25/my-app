FitHouse Mobile-First Fitness Platform

Designed to help gyms manage clients, routines, and training progress through a structured and guided workout experience.

By combining personalized workout plans, progress tracking, and centralized data management, FitHouse improves training consistency, member engagement, and long-term fitness outcomes.

Core Application Features

FitHouse focuses on simplifying gym operations while providing athletes with a clear, structured, and motivating training experience.

Client Profile Management

Centralized storage of client information including personal data, fitness goals, physical metrics, and training history.

Personalized Workout Routines

Custom workout plans assigned per client, structured by days, muscle groups, exercises, repetitions, and rest times.

Progress & Performance Tracking

Tracking of weights, repetitions, time, and training frequency to evaluate progress and optimize future routines.

Mobile-First Experience

Built with Xamarin.Forms to deliver a smooth, native-like experience across Android devices.

Architecture Overview

Design principles and architectural decisions behind the FitHouse platform.

Mobile Architecture

  • Xamarin.Forms MVVM pattern for clear separation of UI and logic
  • Reusable views and components for scalability
  • Service-based communication with RESTful APIs

Backend Architecture

  • ASP.NET Core Web API with layered architecture
  • Controllers, services, interfaces, and repositories
  • SQL Server persistence with stored procedures
